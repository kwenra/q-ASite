College of Arts and Sciences

Course Name Instructor Summary Other Information
Introduction to Dance as a Contemporary Art Form David Marchant Introduction to dance as a creative art form. Students gain an understanding of the body through movement and expression. Open to all students
Principles of Human Anatomy and Development David Strait An anatomy class that covers most of the functional systems of the human body. Offers valuable preparation to any student interested in human biology, health sciences, medicine, and anthropology.

School of Engineering and Applied Sciences

Course Name Instructor Summary Other Information
Web Development Simon Yost A course that explores the process of designing, creating, and publishing a website. This course integrates basic design principles that graphic designers use alongside the HTML and CSS languages. No experience necessary!
Computer Science I Parwatikar A course that teaches the basics of computer programming. Uses the Java language. No experience necessary! Online course with lab and studio sections that one must be present at.
